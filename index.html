Pig Dice

About Pig Dice

Pig Dice is a game played by two players. The first player is expected to roll the dice/play the game and whichever number appers on his/her side greater than one is their score. The player has an option of adding the number to his/her account so that in the end the one who archieves a score of 100 counts or more the first, is the winner of the game. When a player rolls a dice and gets one, the chance automatically goes to the other player.

Rules

  • If the player roles a 1, they score nothing and it becomes the next player's turn.
  • If the player roles any other number, it is added to their turn total and the player's turn conitues.
  • If the player chooses to "hold", their turn total is added to their score and it becomes the next player's turn.
